Services

Mike Stubbs
Architectural Designer

I have over ten years experience in residential extensions and refurbishments across London and beyond. My approach is to create a dialogue with clients in order gain a comprehensive understanding of their requirements and fully develop the project brief. I do not have a house style but rather I believe in tailoring designs to suit clients' individual needs to ensure they are happy with the end result. I offer a free no-obligation initial consultation to discuss your project and following this I can offer a range of architectural services, including:

Feasibility Study
Concept Design
Application for Planning Permission
Building Regulations Approval
Full Technical Design & Specification

cricklewood extension

Contemporary rear extension at a Victorian house in North London providing a modern kitchen and dining space. Working under the employ of Moxy & Co Studio, I produced survey drawings, developed designs for the extension, produced planning submission drawings and a technical design package.

The rear of this South London house was remodeled to provide new kitchen and open-plan living space. Working for Moxy & Co Studio I developed concept designs and produced drawings for planning and building control submission.
