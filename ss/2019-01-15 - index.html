Services

Studio39 is a design consultancy founded by Mike Stubbs. Drawing on over twelve years industry experience, Mike has specialist architectural expertise in residential, commercial and retail projects.
The philosophy of Studio39 to develop designs that respond creatively and intelligently to the brief and context, delivering design excellence and client satisfaction.
Studio39 can offer a range of architectural services including:

Feasibility Study
Concept Design
Application for Planning Consent
Submission to Building Control
Full Technical Design & Specification
Fit-Out and Joinery Design

CV

2018 + . Studio39
Established own design consultancy, working both indepedently and as a design team collaborator on a broad range of architectural projects.
2014 - 2018 . BPTW Partnership, London
Project runner on Heathside & Lethbridge Phase 4 (1). 236 unit mixed tenure housing development in Lewisham, London for Peabody / Ardmore Construction.
Developed the detailed design from RIBA stage 4 through to completion, producing the construction packages for all areas of the building in accordance with NHBC standards, Lifetime Homes, LHDG, Wheelchair Codes, SBD, Code for Sustainable Homes and Building Regulations.
Responsible for all communication with contractor, sub-contractors and design consultants. Coordinated internal design team and project resourcing.
Other notable project involvement at BPTW:
  • Led the production of the construction details package on delivery of a mixed-use development(2) of 83 dwellings and 16 commercial units in the centre of Greenwich for contractor Bouygues.
  • Tender (RIBA stage 3B), MMA & RM submissions (RIBA stage 3A) for 469 dwellings in Lewisham
  • Construction details package for 37 dwellings in Woolwich
  • Capacity Study, Pre-app and Planning Application for 100 dwellings and ground floor commercial units in Charlton
2011 - 2014 . SWA, London
Worked on projects predominantly in the private housing sector. Developed designs from feasibility to detailed technical design and specification stage, producing and coordinating building regs submissions, tender packages and overseeing construction sites.
Coordinated over fifty successful planning and listed building consent applications across London and beyond, producing all documentation and drawings and leading constructive dialogue with planning officers.
